Nyashinski Autobiography

About

Nyashinski is an unwritten story of a Kenyan music star's journey from teenage
rap sensation with Kleptomaniax to a decade-long hiatus working as a truck
driver in the U.S to a triumphant solo comeback with hits like "Now You Know"
proving his lasting legacy in Kenyan music. Born Nyamari Ongegu, his story is
defined by early fame, a period of anonymity and hard work abroad, and a
mature, impactful return to the industry, focusing on lyrical depth and inspiring
themes.
